World Model

A world model is an internal representation of how the world works. It allows an intelligent system—such as a human or an artificial intelligence—to understand, predict, and interact with its environment.

In simple terms, a world model helps answer questions like:

  • What is happening around me?
  • What will happen next?
  • What should I do to achieve a goal?

In AI, world models are used to simulate environments, make predictions, and improve decision-making without needing constant real-world interaction.

Humans also use world models naturally, based on experience and learning, to navigate daily life and solve problems.
